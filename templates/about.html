A little bit about clean.

clean is a project developed to:

  • Create a collaborative online project-based classroom
  • Integrate social media, peer evaluation, and creative design
  • Extend the functionality of programming learning platforms to other subjects
  • Inspire students with an "education"-style complement to "training"-style online offerings

The clean project was developed at the Education Design-A-Thon at MIT.
